A NEW STANDARD
IN PET CARE

Vanity Fur was created to challenge the way pet care looks and feels.

The Gap

We saw a gap — between function and experience. Between basic grooming and true care. Between necessity and luxury.

The Philosophy

Pet owners today don’t see their dogs as just pets — they’re family, companions, and an extension of their lifestyle. Yet the grooming experience hasn’t evolved to match that level of care.

The Mission

So we built something different. Vanity Fur is a luxury grooming and wellness club designed with intention — where every detail, from service to space, reflects quality, consistency, and ease.

CONCIERGE GROOMING DESIGNED AROUND YOUR SCHEDULE, NOT OURS. WE PICK UP, GROOM, AND RETURN.
MAKING CARE PART OF YOUR ROUTINE. THIS IS NOT GROOMING. IT IS MAINTENANCE FOR THE WAY YOU LIVE.
