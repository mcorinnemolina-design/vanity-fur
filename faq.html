Frequently Asked Questions

Do prices vary by dog size?

Yes. Final pricing may vary slightly based on size, coat condition, and temperament.

How often should my dog be groomed?

Most dogs benefit from grooming every 4–6 weeks.

Can I book without a membership?

Yes, a la carte services are available.

What is the real-time grooming experience?

You'll receive updates and visibility into your dog's grooming session for peace of mind.

Do you offer pickup and drop-off?

Yes. Concierge service is available and included in select memberships.
