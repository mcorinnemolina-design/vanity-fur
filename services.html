Signature Groom

$195 – $225

Our most comprehensive offering—crafted for dogs who deserve to look impeccable and feel their absolute best. A fully tailored grooming experience that blends precision styling with restorative care. Each session is customized to your dog’s coat, lifestyle, and comfort.

Recommended For

Full haircuts, breed-specific styling, or a complete coat reset.

Includes
  • Custom haircut & refined styling
  • Deep, coat-specific cleanse & conditioning
  • Expert blow dry & finishing
  • Nail trim & ear care
The Result

A polished, balanced coat with improved skin health, reduced matting, and a noticeably lighter, more comfortable feel.

Luxury Bath & Blowout

$85 – $105

A refreshing reset designed to maintain your dog’s coat between grooms. This service restores softness, shine, and cleanliness—without altering length—so your dog always looks effortlessly put together.

Recommended For

Short-haired breeds or in-between grooming maintenance.

Includes
  • Gentle, coat-specific cleanse
  • Hydrating conditioning treatment
  • Fluff dry & brush out
The Result

A clean, touchably soft coat with reduced odor and light shedding—like a fresh blowout, but for your pup.

De-Shedding Detox

$125 – $150

A targeted treatment to reduce excess shedding and rebalance the coat. Designed to remove loose undercoat while nourishing the skin, this service dramatically minimizes shedding at home.

Recommended For

Heavy shedders and seasonal coat transitions.

Includes
  • Undercoat release & removal
  • Detoxifying cleanse
  • Skin-support conditioning
The Result

Less shedding, healthier skin, and a lighter, more breathable coat.
